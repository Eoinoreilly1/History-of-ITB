Ecology and Biodiversity

Relic Hedges and treelines

The hedgerows in Fingal County account for 1200ha, of “linear woodland” cover.
There is a drive to increase the woodland coverage in Fingal to 7650ha by 2030 (Fingal County Council, 2010)

The Hay Meadow, Lost Habitats

• Over the last 40 years, farming has intensified to meet production demands. As a consequence most of our wildflower-rich meadows have been lost.
• Allowing grass species to grow tall provides shelter and overwintering sites for many ground beetles and spiders.
